Description

Teriparatide injection is a recombinant human parathyroid hormone analog (PTH 1-34). It has an identical sequence to the 34 N-terminal amino acids (the biologically active region) of the 84-amino acid human parathyroid hormone.

Teriparatide has a molecular weight of 4117.8 Daltons.Teriparatide is manufactured using a strain of Escherichia coli modified by recombinant DNA technology.

Teriparatide from LOKA

Is supplied as a sterile, colorless, clear, isotonic solution in a glass cartridge which is pre-assembled into a disposable delivery device (pen) for subcutaneous injection.
