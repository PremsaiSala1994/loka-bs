Description

L-Asparaginase is an enzyme with far reaching practical uses in the field of oncology. We manufacture L-Asparaginase bulk Lyophilized powder that contains the enzyme L-Asparagine amidohydrolase type EC-2, derived from E.coli. L-Asparaginase activity is expressed in terms of International Units according to the recommendation of the International Union of Biochemistry. One International Unit of asparaginase is defined as that amount of enzyme required to generate 1 μmol of ammonia per minute at pH 7.3 and 37°C.

  2. Is manufactured at a capacity of 2000 Mega units per month
  3. Has a specific activity of at least 250 International units per milligram of protein
  4. Is supplied at a minimum order quantity of 10 Mega units.

Description

PEG-L-Asparaginase (Biosimilar of Oncaspar) is an L-asparagine amidohydrolase that is covalently conjugated to monomethoxypolyethylene glycol (mPEG). L-asparaginase is a tetrameric enzyme that is produced endogenously by E. coli and consists of identical 34.5 kDa subunits. Approximately 69 to 82 molecules of mPEG are linked to L-asparaginase; the molecular weight of each mPEG molecule is about 5 kDa. PEG-L-Asparaginase activity is expressed in International Units. One International Unit of L-asparaginase is defined as the amount of enzyme required to generate 1 micromole of ammonia per minute at pH 7.3 and 37°C.

PEG-L-Asparaginase from LOKA

  1. Is indicated as a component of a multi-agent chemotherapeutic regimen for the first line treatment of patients with ALL and specifically for those with hypersensitivity to native forms of L-asparaginase.
  2. Is supplied as a clear, colourless, preservative-free, isotonic sterile Bulk solution in phosphate-buffered saline, pH 7.3.
  3. Is manufactured at a capacity of 100 Mega Units per month
  4. Is supplied at a minimum order of 200 vials (0.75 Mega Units)

ERWINAZE (asparaginase Erwinia chrysanthemi) Bulk lyophilized powder contains an asparagine specific enzyme derived from Erwinia chrysanthemi. L-asparaginase is a tetrameric enzyme consisting of four identical subunits, each having a molecular weight of about 35 kDa. The activity of ERWINAZE is expressed in terms of International Units.

ERWINAZE from LOKA

  4. Is supplied at a minimum order of 5 Mega Units.
